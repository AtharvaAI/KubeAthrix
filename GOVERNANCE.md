# Governance

KubeAthrix uses a maintainer-led, consensus-seeking model. Maintainers review
changes, manage releases, security response, and project direction. Significant
API, CRD, security, or governance decisions require a public design issue and
approval from two maintainers when the project has at least two active
maintainers. Until then, the listed lead maintainer records the rationale and
invites community review.

Maintainer status is earned through sustained, constructive contributions and
may be granted or removed by existing maintainers in a public governance pull
request. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed. Security embargo decisions
may remain private until coordinated disclosure. No contributor may approve
their own release-critical change without another reviewer when another
maintainer is available.
